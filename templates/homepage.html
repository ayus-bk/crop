About This Project

Crop Recommendation System is an advanced Machine Learning initiative developed as a 7th-semester Project Work by Ayush BK, Bisham Kumal, and Dipak Tamang during the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc. CSIT) program, affiliated with Tribhuvan University (TU). Engineered to bridge the gap between traditional agricultural practices and modern data-driven technology, this system empowers farmers with intelligent, predictive decision-making tools. By analyzing critical environmental and subterranean parameters—specifically soil macronutrients (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium), temperature, humidity, rainfall, and pH levels—the system accurately forecasts and recommends the most viable and high-yielding crops for cultivation, ultimately optimizing agricultural productivity.
